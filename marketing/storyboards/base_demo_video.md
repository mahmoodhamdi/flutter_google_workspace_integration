## Production notes
- Record on a real device — emulator gives away the production feel.
- Use clean test data: realistic names ("Alice Chen", "Marcus Johnson"), real-looking events ("Q2 OKR Review", "1:1 with manager"), Drive files with PDFs (not foo.txt).
- Background music: instrumental, neutral, around -16 LUFS.
- Captions burned in (auto-generated then human-corrected) for accessibility.
