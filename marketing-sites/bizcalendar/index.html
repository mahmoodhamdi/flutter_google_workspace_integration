Team calendar overlay

See your teammates' calendars side-by-side. Color-coded by person. Quick toggle to mute irrelevant teammates.

Smart meeting slot finder

"Find a free hour next week" — works across attendees, respects working hours per person, considers time zones automatically.

Working hours per teammate

Remote-first? Set each member's working hours and time zone. The slot finder respects them; no more 2 AM invites.

Time zone display

Every event shows local time AND each attendee's local time. No more "wait, what's that in PST?"

1:1, standup, weekly review, retro — pre-built templates with smart defaults. One tap to schedule.

Meeting cost calculator

Estimate the cost of every meeting based on attendee salaries (configurable per-team). Optional Slack-style "this meeting cost $X" message after each call.
