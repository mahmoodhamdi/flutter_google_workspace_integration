Built for people who actually care about their files

Drive on its own is great storage. DriveVault makes it an archive.

Auto-backup important folders

Pick folders. Set a schedule. DriveVault copies them to an encrypted local archive, with file-level dedup against your existing local copies.

OCR on scanned PDFs

Receipts, contracts, scanned books — full-text search across all of it, even offline. We index on-device, never upload.

Smart deduplication

MD5-checksum-based dedup. That 200MB presentation you have 4 copies of? DriveVault stores it once.

Bulk download as ZIP

Download whole folder trees as one ZIP. Resume on flaky networks. Includes Drive metadata as a manifest.

Storage analytics

See where your Drive space is going — by folder, by file type, by year. Find that 4 GB of old screenshots in seconds.

Maximum privacy

Uses the SAFEST Drive scope: drive.file + drive.metadata.readonly. DriveVault literally cannot see files it didn't create or you didn't explicitly open.
