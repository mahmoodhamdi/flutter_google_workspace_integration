Better meetings start before you join

MeetCompanion runs alongside Meet, not replacing it.

Pre-meeting prep card

30 minutes before each call: agenda template, attendee LinkedIn bios (if shared), last-meeting notes, action items still open. Quick scan = better meeting.

In-meeting notes synced to Drive

Take notes in a clean editor. Auto-saved to a Drive folder per recurring meeting. Searchable across all your past notes.

Auto-recording reminder

The moment a meeting starts, MeetCompanion offers a one-tap toggle to enable recording. No more "wait, who's recording?"

Post-meeting follow-ups

Extract action items from your notes. Auto-create reminders. Optional: email a summary to attendees via gmail.send.

Meeting cost & time tracker

Total weekly meeting time. Estimated cost. Time-spent vs time-prepped ratio. Calendar-wide stats so you can argue for fewer meetings.

Insights from your calendar

"You spend 12 hours/week in meetings". "Wednesdays are your worst meeting day". Useful patterns extracted from your Calendar — fully on-device.
