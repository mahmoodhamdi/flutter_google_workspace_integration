## Discounts (When to Offer Them)

| Scenario | Discount | When to apply |
|----------|----------|---------------|
| Startup with < $1M ARR | 25% | First product license; not bundles |
| Open-source / nonprofit | 50% | Pre-vetted via GitHub repo; no enterprise tier |
| Bundle 2+ verticals | 15% | Cross-sell from a single vertical inquiry |
| Annual commitment on services | 10% | 12-month service retainer paid upfront |
| Referral from existing customer | $250 credit to referrer | Apply automatically |

**Never discount**: enterprise tier (those buyers expect to pay; discount signals weakness)

## Payment Terms

- **Starter kit**: Stripe / Gumroad — instant download on payment
- **Verticals self-hosted**: Stripe + bank transfer (EGP) — source repo access on payment confirmation
- **Verticals white-label / enterprise**: 50% deposit + 50% on delivery (or escrow via Toptal for international)
- **Services**: 50/50 deposit/delivery; for >$10K, can split into 3 milestones
